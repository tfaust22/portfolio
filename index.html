About Me

Trisha Faust graduated Cum Laude from Millersville University with a BSE in English Secondary-Education. She is certified to teach in the state of Pennsylvania and within five years, has taught grades 7-12. Currently, she is working towards her MLIS in Library and Information Science at Drexel University with the goal to become an academic librarian. Outside of teaching and learning, she enjoys reading, writing poetry and short stories, hiking, and kayaking.

"A library is a place where you can live a thousand lives." - Stephen King

Philosophy Statement

As a former educator, I bring a strong philosophy of supporting and serving others into the field of librarianship. I believe that everybody has the right to research, study, read, and learn anything that intrigues thems. My goal is to continue helping others by meeting their needs to fulfill their interests.
